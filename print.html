Print Instructions

Each issue is a mini quarter-fold edition: one sheet folds out to a full-bleed poster and folds down to the cover, wrapping a saddle-stitched full-bleed plate booklet (cover · 12 plates · four coloured spreads · colophon, 24pp). Colour issues print coated (100 lb matte); Jackie 60 Part I (xerox) prints on warm uncoated stock. The spec below is the interior booklet; each issue also links its full layout and poster. Confirm exact trim and the CMYK profile with Linco prepress (prepress@lincoprinting.com) before final files. Press TIFFs (CMYK, 200 ppi, with bleed) are the true press files — available on request.
